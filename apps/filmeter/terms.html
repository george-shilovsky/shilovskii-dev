Summary

Filmeter is a native iPhone light meter for film photography, distributed through the App Store. It runs locally on your device — no accounts, no servers, no telemetry. Reflected and Sunny-16 metering, EV and exposure pairs, calibration, one film profile, and the roll logbook are free. "Pro" features (incident metering, the Zone System, reciprocity, filter & bellows factors, CSV export, and unlimited film profiles) unlock via a Monthly or Yearly subscription or a one-time Lifetime purchase, priced in the App Store and billed by Apple. Filmeter is an estimate, not a laboratory instrument or a guarantee of correct exposure — your exposure decisions are always yours. All your data lives on your device; we never receive it and cannot recover it.
