1. Our Services

Migaja is a clipboard manager application for macOS. It automatically captures items you copy to the system pasteboard, stores them locally on your Mac in a sandboxed SQLite database, and lets you search and recall them later using either keyword (full-text) search or on-device semantic search powered by a bundled multilingual neural model. The App requires macOS 26 (Tahoe) or later on Apple Silicon and is distributed exclusively through the Mac App Store.

7. Subscription, trial, and pricing

Free 7-day trial

  • Migaja includes a free 7-day trial that begins the first time you launch the App. No credit card is required to start the trial.
  • The trial is anchored to your Apple ID's original purchase date for the App, signed by Apple. Reinstalling Migaja does not reset the trial.
  • During the trial, all features (including semantic search) are fully available.

After the trial — what stays free, what requires Pro

  • Free forever, no subscription required: every clip you copy is still captured; the entire history remains searchable by full-text (keyword) search; pinning, soft-delete, retention and exclusion controls, themes, and hotkeys all stay unrestricted.
  • Pro (subscription required): semantic-search results that are only matched semantically (i.e., not also matched by full-text search) are gated. Opening such a result prompts an upgrade.
